# The Global Stage for Next-Gen Innovators
Inspired by one of the world’s most visionary leaders, the Lee Kuan Yew Global Business Plan Competition (LKYGBPC) is one of Asia's most globally representative and exciting deep-tech startup challenges. Every two years, our pitch competition lights up a global stage for next-gen founders to showcase their groundbreaking innovations and connect with the world, fueling their growth.
Powered by Singapore Management University’s Institute of Innovation and Entrepreneurship (SMU IIE), we have fostered a global youth innovation village and launched countless successful ventures solving real-world challenges since 2002.

## Focus Areas
![](https://lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/sites/lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/files/dxpr_builder_images/Untitled%20design%20%2864%29_0.png?fid=15531)
##### **Carbon Tech**
Solutions to capture, utilize, reduce, or store carbon dioxide, aiming to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and leverage data to optimize carbon management, ESG reporting and financing strategies.
![](https://lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/sites/lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/files/dxpr_builder_images/Untitled%20design%20%2861%29_2.jpg?fid=15536)
##### **Climate Tech**
Climate tech utilises climate-resilient infrastructure to reduce emissions and support sustainable development.
![](https://lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/sites/lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/files/dxpr_builder_images/Untitled%20design%20%2862%29_1.jpg?fid=15541)
##### **Energy Transitions**
Advancements in energy generation, storage, transmission, and distribution to facilitate the shift toward renewable sources to enhance grid resilience, optimize energy use, and ensure access to clean energy.
![](https://lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/sites/lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/files/dxpr_builder_images/Untitled%20design%20%2863%29.jpg?fid=15546)
##### **Food & Land Use**
Innovative solutions promoting sustainable farming and alternative protein sources that enhance food security and minimise environmental impact.
![](https://lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/sites/lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/files/dxpr_builder_images/Untitled%20design%20%2865%29_0.jpg?fid=15556)
##### **Green Buildings & Constructions**
Energy-efficient materials, smart technologies, and sustainable designs that reduce resource consumption and emissions.
![](https://lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/sites/lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/files/dxpr_builder_images/Untitled%20design%20%2866%29_0.jpg?fid=15561)
##### **Homeland Security**
Advanced surveillance, AI, and cybersecurity solutions that strengthen national resilience and enhance rapid-response capabilities by safeguarding critical infrastructure and public spaces.
![](https://lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/sites/lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/files/dxpr_builder_images/Untitled%20design%20%2867%29_0.jpg?fid=15566)
##### **Public Health**
Digital health, AI and other tech solutions that boost patient outcomes and enhance healthcare access, preventive care and overall community wellness.
![](https://lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/sites/lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/files/dxpr_builder_images/Untitled%20design%20%2868%29_0.jpg?fid=15571)
##### **Sustainable Materials & Circularity**
Innovative materials and recycling technologies that promote renewable resources, extend product life cycles, and reduce waste, enabling closed-loop systems.
![](https://lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/sites/lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/files/dxpr_builder_images/Untitled%20design%20%2869%29_1.jpg?fid=15576)
##### **Transport & Logistics**
Smarter transport networks based on EV infrastructure and mobility optimisation that optimise efficiency, reduce emissions and streamline logistics operations.
![](https://lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/sites/lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/files/dxpr_builder_images/Untitled%20design%20%2871%29_0.jpg?fid=15581)
##### **Urban Mobility Solutions**
Urban mobility technologies such as autonomous vehicles and AI serve to optimise traffic, reduce congestion, and lower carbon emissions.
![](https://lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/sites/lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/files/dxpr_builder_images/Untitled%20design%20%2872%29.jpg?fid=15586)
##### **Water Solutions**
Innovations in purification, harness real-time monitoring and AI-driven analytics to ensure access to clean water, optimise water usage, detect leaks, and minimise waste.
![](https://lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/sites/lkygbpc.smu.edu.sg/files/dxpr_builder_images/Untitled%20design%20%2874%29.jpg?fid=15596)
##### **Emerging Tech**
Advances in science and technologies addressing urban solutions and sustainability that ultimately have a positive impact on people, health, environment and the planet.

**Shirley Wong** Entrepreneur-in-Residence, SMU Institute of Innovation & Entrepreneurship & Managing PartnerTNF Ventures Pte Ltd
Shirley is a results-driven business leader and entrepreneur with over 30 years of experience in the IT and Technology industry. She currently serves as the Managing Partner at TNF Ventures Pte Ltd, a seed and early-stage venture fund focusing on technology startups.
From 2013 to 2016, Shirley was the Chairman of Singapore Infocomm Technology Federation, now known as SGTech. In 1994, she co-founded Frontline Technologies, which was listed on the Singapore Exchange in March 2001. Under her leadership, the company grew from a team of 8 to a 5000-strong workforce spread across 11 countries in Asia Pacific, before being acquired by British Telecom Global Services in March 2008.
Since 2013, Shirley's focus has shifted to investing in and mentoring technology-based startups. She actively helps startups secure funding and gain market access while remaining passionate about entrepreneurship and innovative technology. Shirley is also the Entrepreneur-in-Residence at Singapore Management University's Institute of Innovation & Entrepreneurship, where she assists in developing a world-class incubation program.
Shirley has been the Chairperson of the President’s Technology Award from 2017 to 2019, recognizing outstanding contributions to R&D in Singapore. Shirley has chaired the Lee Kuan Yew Global Business Plan Competition for the past three editions, a platform that empowers the next generation of innovators from universities worldwide to develop sustainable urban innovation!
In addition, Shirley serves on the boards of Assurity Trusted Solutions, DSO National Laboratories, Netlink NBN, National Kidney Foundation, and Yellow Ribbon Singapore. She is a member of the CAAS authority committee on Cybersecurity and Data Governance, PDPA Advisory, and SIA Engineering Technology Advisory Board. Shirley was recognized as one of Singapore's 100 Women in Tech 2020 for her significant contributions to the tech sector.
